AKAN NAMES

A web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender
outputs their Akan Name. Akan names are derived from the Ghanaian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name
as a 'Day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul
and character of the person.

Here are some Ghanaian Names:

    Female

  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

    Male
